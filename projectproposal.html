Project Proposal

  • What is your website about?

    • My website will be about a learning material of basic Japanese for foreigners. It will contain basic Japanese character and useful Japanese phrase in various daily situation. I am thinking to add small trivia or cultural difference which foreigners may face when they go to Japan.

  • What is the objective of your website?

    • The objective of this website is to teach and show Japanese culture through language. As an exchange student and representative from Japan, my goal is to not only learn US culture but also teach about my country.

  • Who is your target audience?

    • My target audience will be anyone who is interested in Japan and learning Japanese. I hope I can give incentives to know more about Japan for those who are not familiar with Japan.

  • What content do you plan to include?

    1. Basic information about Japan
    2. Basic Japanese characters and pronunciation
    3. Japanese in ordinary life
    4. Japanese at shopping
    5. high level Japanese (not written in the textbook)
